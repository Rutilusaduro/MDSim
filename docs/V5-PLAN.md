**Status:** For player review after V4 ship.

## V5 vision

V4 made the world **playable and exportable**. V5 adds **scale and identity**: franchise chapters, mutators, deeper content pools, and optional creator tools.

---

## Recommended scope

### Must ship

1. **Chapter 4** — "Temple of Appetite" with unique ending
2. **10+ additional group scenes** — roster-specific branching
3. **15+ weekly events** — rival counters, birthdays, holidays
4. **NG+ mutators** — faster rival, double wardrobe, devoted-only dialogue toggle
5. **Patient loyalty arc UI** — beat preview in modal before auto-fire

### Should ship

6. **Custom hire editor** — name, archetype, body type at recruitment
7. **Franchise mode stub** — second clinic slot, shared reputation
8. **Ambient audio loop** — optional break room hum
9. **Room swap** — drag to reorder within same room
10. **Achievement gallery** — share card images

### Deferred

- Cloud saves / API
- Voice acting
- Full rival clinic management sim (separate screen)

## Out of scope (permanent)

- Insurance / billing
- Non-consensual progression
